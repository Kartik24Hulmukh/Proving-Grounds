# 100x Differentiation and Moat

## Product sentence
Every PR should declare what changed and carry independently replayable evidence showing what was demonstrated, contradicted, vacuous, or still unknown.

## Ten-layer differentiation
1. **Two-world claim oracle:** identical probe against exact base/head SHAs.
2. **Vacuity detection:** intended-change evidence passing on base and head proves nothing changed.
3. **Contradiction-first verification:** attempt to falsify claims before confirming them.
4. **Counterfactual evidence strength:** show which plausible wrong patches the evidence rejects.
5. **Evidence boundary:** publish untested branches/risks and inconclusive results.
6. **Neutral verifier:** works with human, Claude, Codex, Cursor, Copilot, Goose, OpenHands output.
7. **Open capsule protocol:** model-, CI-, and forge-independent evidence artifact.
8. **Maintainer policy:** require evidence without banning AI contributions.
9. **Verification benchmark:** rank agents by verifiability, not code style.
10. **Adapter ecosystem:** language, runner, mutant, oracle, agent and report plugins.

## Compounding moats
- Protocol adoption and archived capsules.
- Public fixture/counterfactual corpus.
- Adapter breadth.
- Maintainer policy adoption.
- Agent benchmark history.
- Community expectation: **No evidence, no merge.**

The algorithm is copyable. The standard, corpus, integrations, policies, and historical evidence network are the moat.
