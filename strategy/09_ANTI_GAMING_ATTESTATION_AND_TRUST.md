# Anti-Gaming, Attestation, and Trust

- Canonical artifact manifest with hashes and exact versions.
- Optional Ed25519/DSSE signing and in-toto predicate.
- Separate verifier/checker process from evaluated change.
- Verify fixture and oracle integrity before/after run.
- Hidden sibling fixtures and seeded variants for benchmarks.
- Canary strings for leakage detection.
- Pass^k and consistency for nondeterministic probes.
- Golden verifier suite blocks protocol/rubric regressions.
- Public transparency feed later; hash chains detect modification but not deletion unless externally anchored/monitored.
