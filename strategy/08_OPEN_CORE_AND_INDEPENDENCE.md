# Open-Core Boundary and Independence

## Always open
CLI/verifier, capsule predicate/spec, replay, base/head engine, core adapters, policy schema, fixtures, benchmark methodology.

## Possible paid layers later
Managed isolated execution, private capsule retention, organization policies, historical analytics, private benchmarks, enterprise adapters and procurement exports.

## Independence firewall
Payment may buy compute/storage/support, never a passing verdict or hidden rank. Scoring code cannot import billing/certification modules. Publish corrections and appeals. Do not call the product a certification authority until governance and external validation exist.
